The Founders

Donald Sutherland and Susan Sutherland are the visionary duo behind Cold Stone Creamery. Their partnership began in 1988 in Tempe, Arizona, where they combined their love for ice cream with a revolutionary "mix-in" concept that would change dessert forever.

The Journey

From a Small Shop to Global Phenomenon

Before establishing Cold Stone, the Sutherlands experimented with various ice cream concepts. Their breakthrough came with the idea of mixing custom toppings into premium ice cream on a frozen granite stone—creating a theatrical, personalized dessert experience.

The Cold Stone Experience

The first Cold Stone Creamery opened its doors in 1988, featuring super-premium ice cream made fresh daily and an entertaining "stone mixing" performance. Customers could choose from dozens of mix-ins—everything from fresh fruits to candy bars to brownie chunks— all folded into their ice cream right before their eyes.

Birth of a Dessert Empire

By the early 2000s, Cold Stone had expanded across the United States and internationally. Their goal was simple: deliver the ultimate ice cream experience by combining exceptional quality, endless customization, and show-stopping entertainment.

Design Philosophy

"We believe ice cream should be an experience, not just a treat," says Donald Sutherland. "Every scoop is made to order with the freshest ingredients, mixed with love on our iconic cold stone."

The Cold Stone aesthetic is characterized by:

  • Super-Premium Ice Cream: 12-14% butterfat for ultimate creaminess
  • Endless Customization: Over 30 mix-ins and 20+ ice cream flavors
  • Signature Creations: Expertly crafted combinations like Founder’s Favorite®
  • Fresh Ingredients: Real fruits, nuts, and baked goods mixed in daily
  • Entertainment Factor: Crew members sing for tips and perform mixing shows

Celebrity Following

  • Justin Bieber: Created his own signature creation
  • Taylor Swift: Spotted enjoying Cake Batter™ ice cream

This organic celebrity love proves that when you combine premium ingredients with unlimited creativity and a dash of entertainment, you create something truly special— an ice cream experience worth singing about.

Looking Forward

As Cold Stone continues to grow, the Sutherlands remain committed to their founding principles. Future plans include expanding plant-based and low-calorie options, introducing seasonal flavors, and exploring new mix-in innovations—all while maintaining the entertainment and customization that defines the brand.

"We're not just serving ice cream," Susan reflects. "We're creating moments of joy. Whether it's a child's first birthday cake or a late-night craving, Cold Stone is there to make every scoop unforgettable."
